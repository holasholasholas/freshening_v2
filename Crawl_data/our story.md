## Where It All Began

Freshening traces its roots back to 1994 from a modest shop in Eunos, Singapore. It pioneered the industry’s first individually wrapped wet towels for restaurants under the Smartowel brand, now a mainstay in F&B and hospitality.

The company expanded into healthcare with HospiCare during the SARS outbreak in 2003 and launched personal care brand Zappy soon after, which has since become a household name.

Today, as Asia’s leading wet wipes manufacturer, Freshening upholds ISO-certified quality standards, with products tested in accordance with European Standards, delivering trusted hygiene solutions that enrich lives worldwide.

## An Innovative Force

We have built a stellar in-house Research & Development and Quality Control team, along with three world-class manufacturing facilities in Singapore, Indonesia, and China, to accelerate the development of innovative hygiene solutions.

Our operations are ISO-certified, and our products are tested in accordance with European Standards to ensure the highest quality and safety. We continually refine our knowledge, explore cutting-edge technologies, and challenge the status quo to drive performance, positioning us at the forefront of industry innovation.

## A Mark of Confidence

Over the years, we have developed thousands of wet wipes contract manufacturing and private label products, a testament of our partners’ trust in our quality of work. Our hygiene solutions for businesses encompass a full suite of cleaning and disinfectant products that can be specially formulated to address their facility’s needs.

With more than 5,000 healthcare, food service, airline, and hospitality establishments in our portfolio of clients, and with our products found in over 38 countries globally, we are recognised as a trusted brand and valued partner by renowned organisations worldwide.
